THE GREY HORIZON

Waves crash against the cliff face, relentless in their assault. The ancient stone bears witness to centuries of such fury, unmoved yet slowly yielding to the inevitable erosion of time and tide.

The lighthouse keeper's journal speaks of strange lights beneath the water's surface during the new moon. Many dismiss these as mere hallucinations born from isolation, yet the fishing vessels avoid that particular stretch of coastline with uncharacteristic discipline.
FORGOTTEN DEPTHS

What secrets lie beneath the churning waves? What ancient memories are preserved in the cold darkness where sunlight fears to tread? The ocean keeps its counsel, revealing truth only to those willing to surrender themselves to its embrace.

Three ships have vanished this season alone. The authorities speak of unusual currents and navigational error, yet those who have sailed these waters for generations know better than to accept such convenient explanations.
